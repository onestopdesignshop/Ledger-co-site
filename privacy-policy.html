Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: JUNE 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ledger & Co. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or purchase a membership plan. By using this site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Account information: when you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password.
  • Purchase information: when you buy a membership plan, payment processing is handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy, our merchant of record. We receive confirmation of your purchase, the plan and billing interval selected, and your email address — we do not receive or store your full card number.
  • Email list information: if you sign up for our free email list, we collect the email address you provide.
  • Usage information: we may collect basic, aggregated analytics about how the site is used (e.g. which pages are visited) to understand what content is useful. This does not include personally identifying details beyond what's described above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide access to the membership content and features included in your plan;
  • Send you the monthly content refreshes, mini-guides, and other material included in your plan;
  • Respond to support requests, Q&A submissions, and refund requests;
  • Send free educational content if you've opted into our email list;
  • Maintain account security, including enforcing the single-session policy described in our Terms of Sale;
  • Understand and improve the site and our content over time.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, acting as merchant of record. Lemon Squeezy's own privacy practices govern how your payment card details are handled; we encourage you to review Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy directly. Ledger & Co. never directly receives or stores your full card number.

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

This site may use cookies or local browser storage for basic functionality, such as remembering whether you've already seen a particular popup, or keeping you signed in. We do not use this information for cross-site advertising tracking.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and purchase information for as long as your account remains active, and for a reasonable period afterward as needed for legal, tax, or recordkeeping purposes. If you'd like your account information deleted, contact us using the details in Section 8 and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any retention obligations we may have under applicable law.

6. Sharing of Information

We share information only as needed to operate the site and deliver your membership, including with:

  • Lemon Squeezy — for payment processing, subscription billing, and tax/VAT collection;
  • Service providers we use to host the site, manage email delivery, or provide similar operational functions, under reasonable confidentiality expectations;
  • Legal authorities, if required to comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below and we will respond as required by applicable law.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your information can be sent to support@ledgerco.com.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above; for significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify members directly.
