Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: JUNE 18, 2026 — APPLIES TO ALL LEDGER & CO. PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

This Privacy Policy explains what information Ledger & Co. collects when you visit our website or purchase a membership, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over it. We've written this to be read, not skimmed past.

Ledger & Co. is an educational content publisher. We are not a bank, broker-dealer, or financial institution. We do not collect or store payment card information — all payment processing is handled by Lemon Squeezy, which has its own privacy policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you give us directly

  • Email address — when you sign up for our free rate sheet, create an account, or complete a purchase. This is the primary way we communicate with members.
  • Account credentials — if you create a member account, we store your email address and a hashed (not readable) version of your password. We never store your password in plain text.
  • Q&A submissions — if your membership tier includes written Q&A access, questions you submit are stored so we can respond to them.
  • Value tracker entries — if you use the optional savings tracker inside the member dashboard, the figures you log are stored to your account.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage analytics — we use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not track you across other sites. It collects aggregate page view data (which pages are visited, rough geographic region, device type) without identifying you individually.
  • Browser and device information — standard server logs may record your IP address, browser type, and referring URL. Logs are retained for up to 30 days for security purposes and then deleted.

1.3 Information from third parties

  • Lemon Squeezy — when you complete a purchase, Lemon Squeezy processes your payment and shares with us your email address, the product purchased, billing period, and subscription status. We use this to verify your access tier. We do not receive your full card number, CVV, or bank account details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the guides, rate sheets, and other content included in your membership tier
  • To send monthly content refresh notifications and any Q&A responses
  • To verify your subscription status when you sign in
  • To send transactional emails — receipts, renewal reminders, refund confirmations
  • To respond to support or contact requests you initiate
  • To understand which content is most useful (via aggregate, non-identifying analytics)
  • To comply with legal obligations if required
We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data to serve targeted advertising. We do not share your email address with any third party for their own marketing purposes.

3. Email Communications

By providing your email address — whether through the free rate sheet signup or a paid membership — you agree to receive emails from Ledger & Co. related to that signup. Specifically:

  • Free list subscribers receive the initial rate sheet and occasional educational content updates. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.
  • Paid members receive monthly content refresh notifications, Q&A responses, and transactional emails (receipts, renewal notices). Transactional emails cannot be opted out of while your subscription is active, as they are necessary to deliver the service you paid for.

We do not send promotional emails to paid members on behalf of third parties.

4. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. Plausible Analytics, our analytics provider, operates without cookies entirely. We use browser localStorage only to maintain your session state (i.e., keeping you signed in) while you are actively using the site. This data stays in your browser and is not transmitted to any third party.

5. Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processor and subscription manager. Your purchase data is stored on their platform. See Lemon Squeezy's Privacy Policy.
  • Email delivery provider — we use a transactional email service to deliver membership content and receipts. Your email address is shared with this provider solely for delivery purposes.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Ledger & Co. or its members.
  • Business transfer — if Ledger & Co. is acquired or its assets transferred, member data would transfer to the acquiring party, who would be required to honor this policy or notify you of any material changes.

6. Data Retention

  • Active members — your account data is retained for the duration of your membership and for 90 days after cancellation, to allow for reactivation or dispute resolution.
  • Inactive accounts — if you created an account but never purchased, or if your membership lapsed more than 12 months ago with no activity, we may delete your account data.
  • Financial records — purchase records are retained for 7 years as required for tax and accounting compliance.
  • Email list — free-list subscribers are retained until they unsubscribe. We perform periodic list hygiene to remove long-inactive addresses.

7. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you can:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements for financial records)
  • Unsubscribe — opt out of non-transactional emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at dee8shops@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed password storage, and access controls limiting who within our operation can access member data. No system is perfectly secure — if you have reason to believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

9. Children's Privacy

Ledger & Co. is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If we become aware that we have done so, we will delete that information promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes — meaning changes that meaningfully affect how we use your data — we will notify active members by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the service after the effective date of any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns about this policy:

  • Email: dee8shops@gmail.com
  • Response time: within 5 business days for general questions; within 30 days for formal privacy requests
This policy was last reviewed on June 18, 2026. It covers the Ledger & Co. website at onestopdesignshop.github.io/Ledger-co-site/ and all associated membership services. It does not cover third-party sites linked from our content.
