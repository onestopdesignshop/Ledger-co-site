How it's actually built

A balance sheet that doesn't balance isn't a template — it's a liability.

Most "financial model" products are static mockups. Every model in this suite is built with live formulas, cross-checked, and recalculated until it's correct — not until it looks right.

  • →

    Real formulas, not hardcoded numbers

    Every total, ratio, and projection is a live Excel formula. Change an input and the entire workbook recalculates — nothing is a frozen snapshot.

  • →

    An explicit opening balance sheet

    The 3-statement models open with a true Year-0 position, solved so the books tie from day one — the standard practice institutional modelers use and most templates skip.

  • →

    Cash, equity, and debt fully roll forward

    Net income flows into equity and cash. CapEx flows into both fixed assets and cash. Nothing is double-counted or left dangling between statements.

  • →

    Recalculated and error-checked, not eyeballed

    Every workbook is run through a full formula recalculation and scanned for errors before delivery — across all sheets, every time a module changes.

Questions

Before you buy

What format do I actually receive?+
Each tier is delivered as Excel workbooks (.xlsx) you can open in Excel, Google Sheets, or LibreOffice, plus a companion playbook. Everything is yours to download immediately after purchase — there's no login, dashboard, or subscription involved.
Is this financial advice?+
No. This is organizational and planning infrastructure — structured tools for modeling your own numbers. It isn't personalized financial, tax, legal, or investment advice, and nothing here guarantees any outcome. For decisions specific to your situation, work with a licensed professional.
Can I upgrade from Foundation to a higher tier later?+
Yes — reach out and we'll apply your original purchase price as credit toward Operator or Institutional.
What does the Institutional resale license actually allow?+
You may white-label, rebrand, and resell the toolkits and methodology documentation to your own clients. It does not include rights to resell this landing page, the Ledger & Co. brand, or any of our other products. Full terms are included in the Institutional tier playbook.
Does the trading module include a working trading bot?+
No. Module 16 documents trading-system methodology — signal logic architecture, risk-engine design, and deployment patterns — as a conceptual framework. It does not include live, deployable source code, API credentials, or proprietary parameters tuned to any specific account.
